Services

  • Free 20 min Call

    In this 20-minute call, we’ll discuss your goals, challenges, and current physical and mental condition. Whether you're aiming to boost performance or recover from injury, I’ll assess your needs and explain how NAT can help you move better, feel stronger, and reach your potential.

  • Neuroassessment – Functional Analysis of Your Nervous System

    This assessment provides a detailed analysis of your central and peripheral nervous system function, focusing on visual, vestibular, and proprioceptive integration. By identifying potential inefficiencies or imbalances, we gain valuable insights into how your nervous system influences movement, cognition, and overall performance—laying the foundation for targeted interventions if needed.

  • Personal & Athletic Training – Neurocentric, Goal-Oriented, Effective

    Whether you're looking to improve strength, endurance, coordination, or overall fitness, these training sessions are tailored to your individual goals. Using a neurocentric approach, we integrate targeted exercises that not only train your body but also engage and optimize your nervous system—leading to more efficient, sustainable results.

Neuroathletik Group Workshops – Sharpen the Brain, Elevate Performance

These group workshops offer a powerful introduction to neurocentric training for teams, clubs, and groups across disciplines. By focusing on the brain and nervous system as the control center of movement, Neuroathletiktraining helps participants improve coordination, reaction time, focus, and overall movement efficiency. In a team setting—whether in sports, dance, or performance—this approach builds a shared foundation of body awareness, mental clarity, and injury prevention. For sports teams in particular, the benefits are immediate and measurable: faster decision-making, better on-field communication, quicker recovery, and more resilient athletes. Each workshop is tailored to the specific demands of the group, combining theory with hands-on practice. Participants leave with a deeper understanding of their own nervous system—and tools they can apply in training and competition. Perfect for sports teams, performance groups, dance ensembles, and anyone looking to level up through smarter training.

Diving into Arts / Workshops for Creativity, Productivity & Relaxation – Brain-Based Tools for Modern Life

In these workshops, I guide teams, creatives, and individuals through practical, neuroscience-based tools that help unlock creativity, improve focus, and reduce stress. Whether in a retreat setting or a corporate environment, participants learn how their nervous system influences performance, communication, and well-being—and how to work with it, not against it. Using insights from Neuroathletic Training and movement science, these sessions combine theory with direct experience to create lasting impact. From simple breathing techniques and sensory drills to movement-based practices, the tools are easy to apply in everyday life and work. Ideal for teams seeking innovation, creatives looking for flow, or anyone wanting more clarity and balance in their day-to-day routines.

About Me

Stamatios Papavasiliou
